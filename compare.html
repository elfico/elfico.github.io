Drugs Interactions


How it Works

  1. This module show the interaction between TWO or THREE drugs

  2. Select drugs from the pre-selected values or enter a drug name

  3. Click Add to verify that the drug has a rxcui. Drugs without RXCUI will not be computed

  4. Click Compare to see the interaction of each drug with the other

  5. As an example, use the following drugs: simvastatin, bosentan, fluconazole
