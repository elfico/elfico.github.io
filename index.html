Single Drug Interactions


How it Works
  1. This module list all the interactions of a particular drug
  2. Enter the name of the drug or the RXCUI number of the drug
  3. If you have entered the name, click FIND RXCUI to get the RXCUI of the drug
  4. Click CHECK to see the the interaction of the selected drug with other drugs
  5. As an example, use paracetamol
